About Us

YK Networks builds digital tools and services that help businesses in the Nigerian informal markets grow, scale and create more value. Our digital tools and services are built on three pillars:

  • Seamless and reliable connectivity
  • Borderless payment solutions
  • Discoverability

We are building the ecosystem that will power digital commerce in the informal sector. Our target are our open markets traders, and we are building services and digital assets that make every trader, every small shop owner, every corner shop, every hustler digital-ready.
